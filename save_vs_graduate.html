Save vs Graduate Early

Compare asset growth when doing a PhD: does saving during the PhD matter more, or graduating earlier? Both paths use the same post-PhD salary and savings; only during-PhD savings and graduation year vary.

Formulas (per year) ▼

During PhD (year t < G): salary = stipend. Save path: savings = rate% × stipend or 12×($/month). Not save path: savings = 0. Assett+1 = assett × (1 + r) + savings.

After PhD (year t ≥ G): both paths use the same salary (post‑PhD × growth) and same savings rule. G = graduation year.
